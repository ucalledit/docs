## Welcome to Totalis

Totalis is a request-for-quote (RFQ) marketplace for parlay bets on Kalshi prediction markets. Users create multi-leg parlay bets, market makers compete to offer the best payout odds, and trades are settled via on-chain Solana escrow.

## For Users

Create parlay bets across multiple Kalshi prediction markets and get competitive quotes from market makers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an RFQ">
    Submit a parlay bet request with 2-5 legs and your bet amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Quotes">
    Market makers compete to offer you the best payout odds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept and Settle">
    Accept the best quote. Funds are locked in on-chain escrow and automatically settled when markets resolve.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Market Makers

Monitor incoming RFQs in real-time, submit competitive quotes, and earn the spread.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect via WebSocket">
    Subscribe to the `rfqs:open` channel to receive new RFQs instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Quotes">
    Analyze legs and submit your payout odds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Trades">
    When a user accepts your quote, confirm within 60 seconds to lock in the trade.
  </Step>
</Steps>
